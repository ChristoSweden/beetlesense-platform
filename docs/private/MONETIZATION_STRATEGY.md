## Executive Summary

This document defines BeetleSense.ai's comprehensive monetization plan across five revenue streams: SaaS subscriptions, marketplace fees, API/data licensing, government contracts, and value-added services. The strategy targets 500K SEK ARR in Year 1, 5M SEK in Year 2, and 20M+ SEK in Year 3, with a clear path to profitability by mid-Year 2.

## 4. Pricing Psychology

### 4.1 Why 599 SEK/month for Pro

| Factor | Analysis |
|---|---|
| **Anchored below existing alternatives** | Manual forest inventory costs 500-2,000 SEK/ha. A single 10 ha survey costs 5,000-20,000 SEK. Pro at 599 SEK/month with 5 parcels and unlimited satellite monitoring is dramatically cheaper than any alternative. |
| **Below the "corporate card" threshold** | 599 SEK/month (~60 EUR) is a cost that a forest owner can approve personally without board/committee approval. Removes procurement friction. |
| **Monthly = low commitment** | Forest owners are risk-averse. Monthly subscription lets them try for one month during beetle season. Annual discount (17%) rewards commitment without forcing it. |
| **Comparable to streaming services** | Swedish consumers pay 149-229 SEK/month for streaming. 599 SEK is "two Netflix subscriptions" — a mental model they understand. For a productive tool protecting assets worth millions, this feels affordable. |
| **Value ratio** | Average Swedish forest property value: 2-10M SEK. 599 SEK/month (7,188 SEK/year) to protect an asset worth 2M+ SEK = 0.36% of asset value per year. Forest insurance costs 0.1-0.3% of asset value. BeetleSense at a similar range feels appropriate. |
| **Room to grow** | Starting at 599 gives room to raise prices as value increases (more modules, better AI, more data). Much easier to raise from 599 to 799 than from 999 to 1,199. |

### 4.2 Why 2,999 SEK/month for Enterprise

| Factor | Analysis |
|---|---|
| **B2B decision = different buyer** | Enterprise buyers are timber companies, large estates (1,000+ ha), forestry firms. They evaluate ROI, not personal affordability. |
| **ROI-justified** | Enterprise customer manages 1,000+ ha. Beetle damage costs 500-2,000 SEK/ha. Preventing damage on even 10 ha/year (50K SEK saved) pays for the annual subscription (36K SEK) with margin. |
| **Includes premium features** | Priority 4h SLA, unlimited parcels, API access, white-label AI companion, Module 6. Significant incremental value over Pro. |
| **Below enterprise software norms** | Enterprise forestry software (FORAN, Heureka, timber ERP) costs 5,000-20,000 SEK/month. BeetleSense at 2,999 is positioned as a complement, not a replacement. |
| **Annual contract incentive** | 29,990 SEK/year (17% discount) is a clean number below the 30,000 SEK psychological threshold. Encourages annual commitment for improved cash flow. |

### 4.3 Why 299 SEK/month for Pilot Pro

| Factor | Analysis |
|---|---|
| **Pilots are cost-sensitive** | Drone pilots are often sole proprietors. 299 SEK is affordable as a business expense. |
| **Commission reduction is the real value** | Pilot Free: 15% commission. Pilot Pro: 10% commission. If a pilot does 3+ jobs/month averaging 5,000 SEK, the 5% commission reduction saves 750 SEK/month — more than the 299 SEK subscription. Self-selecting: only active pilots subscribe. |
| **Featured listing drives more jobs** | Verified badge and featured profile position drives more job inquiries. Direct ROI through increased work volume. |
| **Professional tool cost** | 299 SEK is comparable to other professional subscriptions (accounting software, scheduling tools). Familiar price point for Swedish sole proprietors. |

### 4.4 Free Tier Strategy

The Free tier is not charity — it is a customer acquisition engine.

| Purpose | Mechanism |
|---|---|
| **Top of funnel** | Zero barrier to try. Forest owner adds one parcel, sees satellite monitoring, experiences AI companion. |
| **Conversion trigger** | Free tier limits (1 parcel, 10 AI sessions, no drone) create natural friction points. "Upgrade to Pro to add your other 3 parcels." |
| **Data flywheel** | Every free user adds a parcel. More parcels = more data = better ML models = better product for everyone. |
| **Word-of-mouth** | Free users tell other forest owners. Swedish forestry community is tight-knit. Network effects. |
| **Competitive defense** | If Katam offers free smartphone inventory, BeetleSense must match with a free satellite tier. Prevents competitor from owning the entry point. |

## 6. Monetization Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| **Price too high for small owners** | MEDIUM | Free tier ensures accessibility. Pro at 599 SEK tested against willingness-to-pay surveys. Monthly billing reduces commitment risk. |
| **Enterprise sales cycle too long** | HIGH | Government and insurance pilots provide bridge revenue. Value-added services generate immediate cash flow. |
| **Pilot network insufficient** | HIGH | Onboard pilots before launching owner-facing features. Offer pilot incentives (first 3 months free). Target existing drone service companies, not just individuals. |
| **Free tier cannibalizes paid** | MEDIUM | Clear feature differentiation. Free limitations are functional (1 parcel, no drone), not artificial (watermarks, ads). Users naturally need more parcels. |
| **Churn higher than projected** | HIGH | Annual contract discounts. Switching costs (data history, AI companion knowledge). Continuous feature delivery. Customer success for Pro/Enterprise. |
| **Government contract delays** | MEDIUM | Do not depend on government revenue in Year 1 projections. Treat as upside. Focus on SaaS fundamentals. |
| **Currency risk (EUR/SEK)** | LOW | All Swedish-market pricing in SEK. Infrastructure costs partially in EUR — hedge by pricing EU expansion in EUR. |

## 9. Decision Log

Decisions made and rationale for future reference.

| Decision | Options Considered | Chosen | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pricing currency | EUR vs SEK | SEK | Swedish market first. Forest owners think in SEK. Can add EUR for Nordic expansion. |
| Billing model | Per-hectare vs subscription | Subscription with per-hectare for Starter | Subscription = predictable MRR. Per-hectare captures upside for large estates via Enterprise tier. |
| Free tier | Yes vs No vs Freemium trial | Permanent free tier | Swedish market is small and tight-knit. Free tier = word-of-mouth engine. 1 parcel limit ensures natural upgrade path. |
| Annual discount | 10% vs 17% vs 20% | 17% (2 months free) | "2 months free" is a clean, easy-to-communicate offer. Standard in Nordic SaaS. |
| Pilot commission | 10% vs 15% vs 20% | 15% free / 10% Pro | Competitive with other marketplace commissions. Commission reduction incentivizes Pilot Pro subscription. |
| API access | All tiers vs Enterprise only | Enterprise + standalone API tiers | API in Enterprise keeps high-value customers. Standalone API tiers capture non-forestry buyers (insurance, research). |
