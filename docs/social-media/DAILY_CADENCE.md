## Monday: Research Spotlight

**What**: Pick ONE peer-reviewed paper relevant to Nordic forestry. Break down the key finding in plain language. Always cite properly.

**Source pool**: SLU publications, EFI policy briefs, Forest Ecology and Management journal, Scandinavian Journal of Forest Research, IPCC forestry chapters, Skogsstyrelsen annual reports.

**Template**:
```
🔬 [Surprising finding in one sentence]

[2-3 sentences explaining what the researchers found and why it matters for forest owners]

The science: [1 sentence on methodology]
The implication: [1 sentence on what forest owners should do differently]

📄 [Full citation — Author (Year). Title. Journal.]

#BeetleSense #ForestScience #ForestIntelligence #[topic tag]
```

**Image**: Clean infographic showing the key data point. Forest green + white palette. One chart or diagram. Source citation visible. BeetleSense watermark.

**Recurring topics** (rotate monthly):
- Bark beetle phenology & climate
- Carbon sequestration methodology
- Remote sensing in forestry
- Biodiversity metrics
- Fire ecology in boreal forests
- Growth modeling advances
- Drought-tree defense interactions
- GEDI/LiDAR canopy measurement

## Platform-Specific Adaptation

### LinkedIn (primary — professional audience)
- Full post text (up to 3,000 chars)
- Image or document carousel
- Tag organizations: @EFI, @SLU, @Skogsstyrelsen, @Copernicus
- Engage in comments with additional data points
- Post at 08:00 CET (Swedish morning commute)

### X/Twitter (secondary — tech/science audience)
- Condensed to 280 chars + thread if needed
- Same image
- More hashtags
- Post at 14:00 CET (global reach)

### Instagram (tertiary — visual showcase)
- Image-first, caption second
- Stories for quick data pulses
- Carousel for checklists and explainers
- Post at 18:00 CET (evening scroll)

## Content Production Workflow

1. **Sunday evening**: Draft next week's 7 posts (use this template)
2. **Generate images**: Use NotebookLM or NanoBanana with the image prompts
3. **Schedule**: Use Buffer, Hootsuite, or LinkedIn's native scheduler
4. **Engage**: Respond to comments within 4 hours on weekdays
5. **Measure**: Track weekly: impressions, engagement rate, profile visits, website clicks
6. **Iterate**: Double down on what works. Drop what doesn't. Review monthly.
