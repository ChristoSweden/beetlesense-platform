# BeetleSense Social Media Content Strategy

## Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Authoritative but accessible. Science-backed, not academic.
- **Style**: "Here's what the research says, and here's what it means for your forest."
- **Language**: English primary (LinkedIn global reach), Swedish secondary (local engagement)
- **Never**: Hype, buzzwords, fear-mongering. Always cite sources.

## Content Pillars (rotate daily)

| Day | Pillar | Theme | Example |
|-----|--------|-------|---------|
| Monday | 🔬 Research Spotlight | Highlight a peer-reviewed paper | "New SLU study shows bivoltine beetle activity in southern Sweden..." |
| Tuesday | 🛰️ Satellite Intelligence | Explain a remote sensing concept | "How NDVI anomaly detection catches beetle damage 3 weeks before visual symptoms..." |
| Wednesday | 🌲 Forest Owner Tip | Practical forestry advice | "When to inspect your spruce stands: the GDD threshold checklist..." |
| Thursday | 🤖 AI in Forestry | Show Wingman capabilities | "We asked our AI Knowledge Wingman about carbon accounting — here's what 2,000+ papers say..." |
| Friday | 📊 Data Insight | Share a data-driven finding | "Community data shows beetle trap counts 62% above baseline in Kronoberg this week..." |
| Saturday | 🌍 ForestWard / EU Policy | Regulatory or pan-European angle | "How the EU Deforestation Regulation affects Swedish forest owners..." |
| Sunday | 💡 Behind the Build | Engineering/product story | "We cross-validate NDVI from 3 satellites. Here's why that matters..." |

## Post Format
- **Hook line** (1 sentence, attention-grabbing)
- **Insight** (2-3 sentences, the substance)
- **Source citation** (paper/data reference)
- **Call to action** (try BeetleSense, follow for more, comment)
- **Hashtags** (5-7 relevant)
- **Image prompt** (for NotebookLM/NanoBanana visual generation)
