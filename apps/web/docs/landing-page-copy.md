## Feature Section 1: Satellite Monitoring

### Heading
Your forest. Watched every 5 days.

### Body
Sentinel-2 satellites pass over Sweden continuously. BeetleSense reads the spectral data from your specific parcels and calculates a beetle risk score — updated every 5 days, automatically. No site visits. No guessing.

### Visual suggestion
Split screen: satellite image of forest (healthy green) vs. same parcel with risk overlay (amber/red zones highlighted)

### Heading

## Feature Section 3: Survey Workflow

### Heading
From satellite alert to verified ground data.

### Body
When BeetleSense flags a risk, you can order a drone survey directly from the platform. Certified pilots in your region accept the mission, fly, and upload results — all in one place. You get verified beetle presence data, not just satellite estimates.

### Visual suggestion
3-step flow: Satellite alert → Mission accepted by pilot → Results in dashboard
