# BeetleSense — User Personas

## Persona 1: Gunnar, 58 — The Part-Time Forest Owner

**Role**: Forest owner (ägarrollen)
**Forest size**: 85 hectares in Dalarna, inherited from his father
**Goal**: Protect the value of his forest from bark beetle spread without spending every weekend in the field
**Primary frustration**: "I only find out my trees are dead when I visit in the summer. By then it's too late."
**Device preference**: iPhone — uses it in the field and at the kitchen table. Not comfortable with complex desktop software.
**Tech comfort**: Medium. Uses BankID, Skatteverket app, and Hemnet. Intimidated by dashboards with too many options.
**Language**: Swedish preferred

**Behavioural patterns**:
- Checks the app when he's concerned after storms or warm summers
- Will not read long onboarding tutorials — needs immediate value
- Trusts the platform if it shows him his actual parcel, not a generic map
- Will refer the app to his hunting club if it works well

**First Win**: Registers his parcel by fastighets-ID and sees his forest highlighted on the map with a current beetle risk score — in under 2 minutes.

## Persona 2: Maja, 31 — The Certified Drone Pilot

**Role**: Pilot (pilotroll)
**Background**: Certified drone operator, runs surveys for 4–5 forest owners per month as a side business alongside her forestry consultant day job
**Goal**: Fill her survey calendar, complete missions efficiently, and get paid faster
**Primary frustration**: "I spend more time on admin — emails, PDFs, invoices — than I do flying. I need one place for everything."
**Device preference**: Android tablet in the field, MacBook for planning. Needs a fast mobile interface for on-site data upload.
**Tech comfort**: High. Early adopter, uses Notion, Airtable, and drone management software.
**Language**: Swedish, comfortable with English UI

**Behavioural patterns**:
- Checks the job board daily
- Uploads data immediately after missions from the field
- Wants clear status on processing queue — hates uncertainty
- Will leave a platform if invoicing is manual

**First Win**: Sees available survey missions near her location on the job board and submits an application — in under 2 minutes from login.

## The First Win (Both Personas)

**Definition**: The moment a new user feels the product is working for them specifically.

- **Forest owner**: Their parcel appears on the map with a real risk score. This is personal and immediate — it replaces months of anxious uncertainty with a number and a colour.
- **Pilot**: A real mission appears on the job board with enough detail to assess fit. This replaces email back-and-forth with a single tap.

**Target**: Both wins achievable in under 2 minutes from signup (or demo mode activation).

**How to protect this**:
- Demo mode must show a realistic parcel (not an empty state) with sample data
- The onboarding flow must surface the parcel map or job board as step 1
- Never show an empty dashboard to a first-time user — always load demo data as the default state
